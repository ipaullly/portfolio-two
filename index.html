I was on cusp of graduating with a BSc. Mechatronics late in 2017 when curiosity led me to web development. My initial motivation was driven by getting a job while also learning more about best practices but it gradually morphed. I learnt code basics with python courtesy of traversy media and pretty printed youtube channels and was my language of choice during the first year of code. After getting more adept in software development with all its intricacies I transitioned into JavaScript and the bright waters of Frontend. the backend marvels me with how critical infrastructure can be built, maintained and scaled to handle thousands of requests in seconds, but it's colors and shapes that appeal to my artistic heart and I've never looked back since. I enjoy design prototyping, building templates with HTML/CSS and working with frameworks like Angular and React. In my spare time I dabble in HTML5 game development, and building simple CMS sites with Wordpress. Non-code interests include gaming, taking walks and socializing with friends
